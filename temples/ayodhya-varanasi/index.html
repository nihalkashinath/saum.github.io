Itinerary Highlights

Thoughtfully planned schedules to experience the most sacred darshans and rituals with minimal crowds.

(If you only want to visit one of these two temples, we can structure the ideal package for that as well.)

Day 1 · Ayodhya

Darshan at Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Mandir, visits to Hanuman Garhi, Kanak Bhawan & Dashrath Mahal. End the evening with Sarayu Aarti by the ghats.

Day 2 · Varanasi

Travel to Kashi and visit Kashi Vishwanath Mandir, Annapurna Devi Mandir, and Vishalakshi Shakti Peeth. In the evening, witness the Ganga Aarti from an elevated vantage point.

Day 3 · Sunrise on the Ganga

Begin with a sunrise boat ride, followed by visits to Durga Kund, Sankat Mochan Hanuman Mandir, and Sarnath before departure. Optional time for pujas, shopping, or food trails.
